A crispy, chewy snack straight from the night markets of Taiwan!

Ingredients

  • 300g sweet potatoes (orange/yellow variety)
  • 50g tapioca starch
  • 30g sugar
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Peel and steam sweet potatoes until soft (about 15 minutes).
  2. Mash them while warm, then add sugar and mix.
  3. Add tapioca starch and knead into a dough.
  4. Roll into small balls (about marble size).
  5. Heat oil in a pot, then deep fry until golden and puffed up.
  6. Drain excess oil and serve hot!
